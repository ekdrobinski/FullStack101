Interests


Despite Minnesota's short growing season, my favorite past-time is gardening. This season, my goal is to be able to grow enough flowers for a friend's wedding in August. In the image above are some tester seedlings for Spring 2023. Included are basil, snapdragons, bachelor's buttons, and cilantro.

Community is an important value of mine. I set aside time in my weekly routine to paint greeting cards for my friends. It's important to me to maintain my friendships. The connections I made as a teacher for over 3 years with co-workers and students are invaluable to me. I learn so much just by interacting with the people around me.

If you walk by me at any time of day, you will be overcome by the aroma of a fresh-brewed coffee. I drink coffee all day long. To be honest, I'll travel hours just to have a new coffee experience. I've traveled to Seattle, WA just to see the Starbucks Reserve and to Guatemala, hiking up a volcano amongst ripe coffee berries.
